Terms & Conditions

Important information about our funeral plans and services

Waiting Periods

Standard waiting period: 3 months for all natural causes

Suicidal causes: 12-month waiting period applies

Transfer benefit: No waiting period if you transfer from another funeral parlour after 3 months of membership

Payment Terms

Important: No claims will be processed if your account is in arrears

Late payment fines:

• R500 fine for 1 month overdue

• R1,000 fine for 2 months overdue

Monthly premiums: Must be paid by the 7th of each month

Coverage Details

Main member: Full funeral service as per selected plan

Spouse: Same benefits as main member

Dependants: Children under 21 or students under 25

Extended family: Parents and in-laws can be added

Policy Information

Required documents: ID copies for all members

Changes: Notify us within 30 days of any changes

Cancellation: 30 days written notice required

Claims: Must be reported within 48 hours
